About Made By Maa

MadeByMaa Seller

If you want to be a next Master Mom Chef by joining MadeByMaa, then you have to first go to Google Play Store and download the MadeByMaa Seller app. After downloading the app, you have to fill your details in it. After this, a team from MadeByMaa arrives at the home chef (yours). MadeByMaa's team collects food quality, hygiene,cleanliness and other things in your home. If everything is found right in your house then you will have to register with MadeByMaa.

In this, the registration number has to be obtained from the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). After this an agreement is signed between the Home Chef and MadeByMaa. You will make the menu, the home chef will decide about the food menu served to the customers. You will also be free to decide the rate of your meal.
