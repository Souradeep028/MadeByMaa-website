About Made By Maa

Are you a mother/woman with a passion for food and love cooking? Is there someone in your family like your mother, sister, or a woman you know who cooks scrumptious meals or other food items like Briyani, bakery items, sweets etc, Then there is great news for you all. You can put your cooking skills to good use and even earn money while sharing your love of food with others. MadeByMaa gives new strength to the Culture of Home-Made Food where you get the opportunity to sell delicious and a variety of cuisines easily in a home plan set up. We offer you to make your unique identity all the while generating income, work on your schedule and preferred hours. So, start your journey to become self-employed today with MadeByMaa. Share with your mother, sister or any female friend with a passion for food and change their life for good.

MadeByMaa Seller

If you want to be a next Master Mom Chef by joining MadeByMaa, then you have to first go to Google Play Store and download the MadeByMaa Seller app. After downloading the app, you have to fill your details in it. After this, a team from MadeByMaa arrives at the home chef (yours). MadeByMaa's team collects food quality, hygiene,cleanliness and other things in your home. If everything is found right in your house then you will have to register with MadeByMaa.

In this, the registration number has to be obtained from the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). After this an agreement is signed between the Home Chef and MadeByMaa. You will make the menu, the home chef will decide about the food menu served to the customers. You will also be free to decide the rate of your meal.

How to use Rodo?
Rodo follows a few simple steps to get you going on
India's first E-Bicycle sharing platform!

Download the Rodo app from the Playstore or the iOS App Store

Wait no more and get the latest version of the Rodo app from the Google Playstore or the iOS App store to get you going on an electrifying ride on India's first public ebicycle sharing platform!

Locate and Unlock

Look for availabe bikes nearby via the Rodo app then simply scan the QR code present on the cycle or enter the Serial number to unlock the bike.

End or Pause your Trip

Park the bike after clicking end trip on the bike and lock it or Pause the trip and park the cycle nearby to attend some quick business.
